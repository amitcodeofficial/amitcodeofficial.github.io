Terna Engineering College

About

Established in 1991 and situated in Nerul, Navi Mumbai Terna Engineering College is approved by the AICTE and the Directorate of Technical Education in Maharashtra. Accredited with NAAC A grade, the College is affiliated to the University of Mumbai. Terna Engineering College has secured 40 position among the top private Engineering colleges in India by Times Ranking 2020.The college offers a total of four programmes such as Bachelor of Engineering, Master of Engineering, research programme in Engineering and PGDM with various specialisations, including Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Hospital and Health Management, Personnel and Human Resource Management, Marketing and Finance, etc. The College also has nine departments.

Terna Engineering College courses can be divided into four programmes such as Bachelor of Engineering, Master of Engineering, research programme in Engineering and PGDM. Top courses offered by Terna College of Engineering are BE in Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering, Civil Engineering, ME and PhD in Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and Information Technology. Also, TEC offers PGDM in Hospital and Health Management, Personnel and Human Resource Management, Rural Management, Marketing and Finance, Sustainability Management. Terna engineering college arranges a one-month internship for BE students through training and placement cell.
